# 需要补充实验的意见整理


## 一、来自 `退修的意见ScholarOne Manuscripts.pdf`

### 1. 审稿人 1 - 关于目标设定鲁棒性与 `\sigma_k` 敏感性

**Original**

1. The effectiveness of the ROI-focused modeling hinges on the specification of the target $𝑦^*$, which is assumed to be known a priori from design requirements. This assumption may limit applicability in scenarios where such targets are not well-defined. The authors are encouraged to discuss the robustness of the method to misspecification of $𝑦^*$. In addition, the bandwidth parameter $\sigma_k$ , currently determined via a heuristic, could potentially be treated as a learnable parameter and optimized jointly. Also how sensitive is the model performance to $\sigma_k$?

**中文翻译**

1. 该方法对感兴趣区域（ROI）进行重点建模，其有效性依赖于目标值 $y^*$ 的设定，而文中假定这一目标值可由设计需求预先确定。在目标值并不明确的应用场景中，这一假设可能会限制方法的适用性。建议作者讨论当 $y^*$ 设定不准确时，方法的鲁棒性如何。此外，目前带宽参数 $\sigma_k$ 是通过启发式方式确定的，理论上也可以将其作为可学习参数并进行联合优化。模型性能对 $\sigma_k$ 的敏感性如何？

### 2. 审稿人 2 - OFDM 案例中需要显式噪声因素与传递方差分析

**Original**

2. In engineering case study in Section 4, the problem setting of OFDM system completely ignores actual noises factors. It is recommended to explicit define noise factors in input variables and investigate how this transmitted variance be calculated and optimized in the proposed method. The current expected quality loss given in Eq. (32) is invalid for robust parameter design of OFDM systems.

**中文翻译**

2. 在第 4 节工程案例研究中，OFDM 系统的问题设定完全忽略了实际噪声因素。建议作者在输入变量中明确界定噪声因子，并研究在所提方法中如何计算和优化这种传递方差。目前公式（32）给出的期望质量损失并不适用于 OFDM 系统的鲁棒参数设计。

### 3. 审稿人 2 - 网络深度与复杂度敏感性分析

**Original**

3. The manuscript omits the exact network depth in the Experimental Setup, though Figures 1 and 2 imply a 3-layer architecture. This missing detail is critical, as a deep structure trained on only 400 samples for four-dimensional inputs risks severe overfitting or dimensionality collapse. It is necessary to specify the depth used and provide a sensitivity analysis justifying this complexity over simpler, shallower alternatives.

**中文翻译**

3. 稿件在 Experimental Setup 中遗漏了网络深度这一关键信息，尽管图 1 和图 2 暗示其为 3 层架构。这个缺失细节非常重要，因为对于四维输入、仅有 400 个样本的数据而言，采用深层结构训练存在严重过拟合或维度塌缩的风险。作者有必要明确说明所采用的网络深度，并提供敏感性分析，以论证为何这种复杂结构优于更简单、更浅层的替代方案。

### 4. 审稿人 2 - 增加独立 GP / 深度 GP 基线对比

**Original**

4. Given the four-dimensional input and the sample size of 400, the design space is relatively well-covered. It is necessary to include independent heterogeneous (or heteroscedastic) GPs or independent deep GPs as baselines. Since separate modeling inherently eliminates task conflicts without complex weighting, these baseline comparisons are essential to justify the necessity of a complex joint architecture.

**中文翻译**

4. 考虑到输入维度为 4 且样本量达到 400，设计空间已经得到了相对充分的覆盖。因此，有必要加入独立异质（或异方差）GP 以及独立深度 GP 作为基线模型。由于分别建模本身就可以在不依赖复杂加权机制的情况下消除任务冲突，因此这些基线对比对于论证复杂联合架构的必要性至关重要。

### 5. 审稿人 2 - 补充时间复杂度与训练耗时比较

**Original**

5. The bilevel optimization and Hessian-vector approximations (Eqs. 24-25 in Pages 13-15) risk training instability and significant overhead on small, noisy datasets. Given that surrogate modeling requires high efficiency, a formal time complexity analysis and a wall-clock training time comparison against static-weighting baselines are required to justify this complex approach.

**中文翻译**

5. 双层优化及 Hessian-vector 近似方法（第 13 至 15 页中的公式（24）至（25））在小样本、含噪数据集上可能带来训练不稳定和显著的额外开销。鉴于代理建模通常要求较高效率，作者需要给出正式的时间复杂度分析，并与静态加权基线进行实际训练耗时比较，以证明这种复杂方法的合理性。

### 6. 审稿人 2 - 增加更强 GP 专用基线

**Original**

6. The Section 4.1 baselines are generic deep learning alternatives. To demonstrate superiority, the framework shall be benchmarked against advanced GP-specific models, including Multi-Task GP (e.g., using the Linear Model of Coregionalization), standard deep GPs for multiple responses and heteroscedastic GPs for multiple responses—the traditional state-of-the-art for non-stationary modeling in industrial engineering.

**中文翻译**

6. 第 4.1 节中的基线方法大多只是通用深度学习替代方案。若要证明所提方法的优势，框架应与更先进的 GP 专用模型进行基准比较，包括多任务 GP（例如使用 Linear Model of Coregionalization）、用于多响应建模的标准深度 GP，以及用于多响应建模的异方差 GP，这些才是工业工程中非平稳建模的传统前沿方法。

### 7. 审稿人 2 - 增加消融实验

**Original**

7. To comprehensively clarify the effectiveness of the proposed method, the ablation study shall be added to decouple the performance contributions of the use of deep structure from those of the double attention mechanisms.

**中文翻译**

7. 为了更全面地说明所提方法的有效性，应补充消融实验，将深层结构本身带来的性能收益与双注意力机制带来的性能收益区分开来。

### 10. 副编辑 - 增加 `\sigma` 的数值敏感性分析

**Original**

As the authors themselves pointed out, 𝜎 is a preset kernel bandwidth. While the problem of estimating this parameter from data might be relegated to future research, the authors should at least present a numerical sensitivity analysis study to show how sensitive or insensitive the results reported in the manuscript are to changes in 𝜎.

**中文翻译**

正如作者自己所指出的，𝜎 是一个预设的核带宽参数。尽管从数据中估计这一参数的问题可以留待未来研究，但作者至少应给出一项数值敏感性分析，以说明文中结果对 𝜎 的变化究竟是敏感还是不敏感。

### 11. 副编辑 - 补充标准差或置信区间

**Original**

Much of the results in Section 4 are presented without reporting their standard deviations. So, it is not clear if some differences are real or just due to random variations. The authors should include standard deviations or confidence intervals for their numerical results.

**中文翻译**

第 4 节中的许多结果在呈现时没有报告标准差，因此无法判断其中一些差异究竟是真实存在的，还是仅仅由随机波动造成的。作者应为数值结果补充标准差或置信区间。

### 12. 副编辑 - 增加不同训练样本规模下的实验

**Original**

The training sample sizes used in the manuscript are quite large. The paper uses a 400-point design for a numerical example with 5 scalar inputs and for the OFDM experiment with 4 scalar inputs. Typical Gaussian process surrogates are fitted with only 10 times the number of scalar inputs. So, the authors should remove their claim about their model improving prediction accuracy in critical subspaces under “small sample conditions” in page 4 and elsewhere. The authors should also show how well the proposed method performs under varying numbers of training samples to illustrate the minimum sample size that is needed for the proposed surrogate to predict well.

**中文翻译**

本文所使用的训练样本规模其实相当大。论文对一个具有 5 个标量输入的数值例子，以及一个具有 4 个标量输入的 OFDM 实验，都采用了 400 点设计。典型的高斯过程代理模型通常只需要大约输入维度数的 10 倍样本进行拟合。因此，作者应删除其在第 4 页及其他位置关于模型在 “small sample conditions” 下提升关键子空间预测精度的表述。同时，作者还应展示在不同训练样本规模下该方法的表现，以说明所提代理模型达到良好预测性能所需的最小样本量。
